Connect your HubSpot

Run the AgentSnap demo against your real HubSpot data. Read-only, browser-only — your token stays in this tab.

🔒 Privacy: Your token stays in this browser tab and is never stored on AgentSnap's servers. Requests proxy through a stateless Cloudflare Worker that does not log tokens. Close this tab and your token is gone.
How do I get a HubSpot private-app token?
  1. In HubSpot, click the settings gear (top right).
  2. Left sidebar: Integrations → Private Apps.
  3. Click Create a private app, name it "AgentSnap Demo".
  4. On the Scopes tab, enable these read-only scopes:
    • crm.objects.contacts.read
    • crm.objects.deals.read
    • crm.schemas.contacts.read
  5. Click Create app at the top right.
  6. Copy the access token (starts with pat-na1-…) and paste it below.
